About Me

I’m a Data Scientist with a strong foundation in analytics and hands-on experience across applied research and machine learning projects. My background includes industry work with large-scale datasets, where I built SQL-based reporting pipelines, analyzed pricing and operational data, and partnered with cross-functional teams to improve data quality, metrics, and decision-making. During my Master’s in Data Science at Rochester Institute of Technology, I expanded into predictive modeling, multimodal deep learning, and rigorous model evaluation, while also supporting analytics workflows and reporting as a graduate assistant. I’m particularly interested in building data systems and models that are reliable, well-evaluated, and genuinely useful, translating complex data into insights that teams can trust and act on.
